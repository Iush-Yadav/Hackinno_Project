About us

Fulhera, a picturesque town nestled in the heart of Odisha, India, derives its name from the Sanskrit words "ful" (meaning flower) and "hera" (meaning abode). This evocative name reflects the town's natural beauty, characterized by vibrant flora and lush greenery. The name also symbolizes a peaceful and harmonious abode, a place where people can flourish and thrive. Fulhera's name encapsulates its idyllic charm and serves as a reminder of its rich cultural heritage and natural splendor.
